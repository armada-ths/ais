    Logistics and booth details

    Is your booth ready?

    Armada will continue the cooperation with Sture Exhibitions & Events AB regarding the construction of stands, rental of furniture and other career fair related services. Sture is one of the largest and most creative companies in Sweden within the field of expo marketing.

    Aligned with Armada's focus on environmental sustainability, we are very proud to collaborate with Sture, a company that has received a gold rating from Svensk miljöbas thanks to their work to reduce their environmental impact. Sture are more than happy to help you with stand designs for both rental and purchase as well as graphic solutions (texts, signs, roll­ups etc.).

    You can find Sture's product catalogue for Armada here. For more information, please visit Sture's website.
    To facilitate the ordering process, please send an e-mail to andreas.tenggren@sture.se and state your request.


    Transportation of your goods to and from the fair

    This year Armada is proud to present the concept Armada Transport which includes free transportation (limitations apply, see the TRANSPORT tab) of your goods to and from the fair. Your goods will be placed at your booth spot upon your arrival. The background behind offering this service is our aim to reduce Armada's impact on the environment and ensure a smooth exhibitor experience. We believe that it is more sustainable to have one transportation company transporting goods with a fewer amount of trucks driving on the road, rather than letting everyone find their own transportation solution leading to a higher climate impact with more trucks driving to KTH and more complex logistics.

    Armada is happy to announce that we have hired DHL to handle the transportation. All of the administration of bookings will be handled directly by DHL. Please note that no exhibitors are allowed to use a different transportation company than DHL. No other company than DHL will get access to KTH's loading platforms and exhibitors who decides to not use Armada Transport will have to carry their goods into the buildings at the fair.

    Please see the TRANSPORT tab here in the registration form for more information regarding Armada Transport. The booking system for transports is now open and is made by filling in this PDF-form and emailing it to tfe.sweden@dhl.com. Deadline for bookings is 4th of November. Reach out to transport@armada.nu for general questions and directly to DHL for booking questions.
