Welcome!

The complete registration for THS Armada 2017 is open between 1st of July 00:01 and 4th of September 23:59

Make sure the form is completed and sent in before the last date of registration. It is possible to start with the form, save it by clicking "Save" in the bottom menu, and then continue later. The questions in the form are divided into 10 pages and you can fill out the information in any order you like. Questions marked with (*) are mandatory.

Some information in this form may be copied from your profile. Please check this information so that it is updated for THS Armada 2017. Your registration is completed when you click on the button "Submit" in the right bottom corner. When you have submitted the application you will receive a confirmation e­mail as soon as the application has been approved.

Please note that the complete registration is binding and you will be liable for all additional services chosen, provided that Armada is able to supply your organisation with your choices.

The Banquet

Welcome to the Grand Banquet of THS Armada!

November 21st Annexet, Globen Dark suit

This is one of the biggest university banquets in Sweden where you and your organization will have the wonderful opportunity to network with many ambitious students in a casual atmosphere. The evening will start off with a refreshing drink and continue with a three course menu and spectacular entertainment.

You will be contacted in October to specify the names of the participants and any special dietary requirements. This information will be gathered before October 27th.

Tables 9,900 / 12,900 SEK

New for this year is that you also have the opportunity to purchase entire tables to the banquet. These tables will have 8 seats, a favorable placement and be branded with your organization logo. You will have the unique chance to handpick top students of your choosing to this table, which will give you a great chance to deepen your connection with interesting top students that you have met at events, at the fair or to get to know new students that have shown great interest in your organization.

Prices:

  • Including two tickets from the base kit: 9,900 SEK
  • Excluding two tickets from the base kit: 12,900 SEK

Banquet ticket - Exhibitor representative 1,190 SEK

This year we have chosen to include 2 banquet tickets in the base kit for each participating exhibitor. Of course you also have the opportunity to purchase additional tickets if you're so inclined.

Banquet ticket - student 1,190 SEK

Did you meet any interesting students during the first day of the fair? Let these students know more about your organisation and get to know them personally in a different setting by inviting them to the banquet. A ticket to the banquet can also be used as a very appreciated prize for students in a possible contest at your stand.

FURNITURE FROM STURE

THS Armada will continue the cooperation with Sture Exhibitions & Events AB regarding the construction of stands, rental of furniture and other career fair related services. Sture is one of the largest and most creative companies in Sweden within the field of expo marketing.

Aligned with THS Armada’s focus on environmental sustainability, we are very proud to collaborate with Sture that has received a gold rating from Svensk miljöbas due to their work to reduce their environmental impact. Sture are more than happy to help you with stand designs for both rental and purchase as well as graphic solutions (texts, signs, roll­ups etc.).

In a separate email, you will receive three complete fair solutions put together exclusively from Sture’s furniture catalogue. To facilitate the ordering process, just click the link to Sture’s dedicated email address below and state your request. For more information visit Sture’s homepage

Thanks to our partnership with the talent network Nova and their deep knowledge within custom events for top talents, you can now get an exponentially greater value from you participation!

From the evaluations of previous years, it can be highlighted that:

  • Many students are not pleased with the company’s choices of representatives.
  • Many student feel they don’t receive the information they want about potential employers.
  • Many companies feel that it is hard to create a value after participating at the career fair.

Nova has done research in collaboration with THS Armada on its specific audience and created a toolkit for solving these problems. We can offer an exclusive service, consulting you in how to get the most from participating in THS Armada.

Price: 10 000 SEK (Valued at 30 000 SEK)

  • START UP MEETING – 1 H

    • Start-up meeting with the main responsible at your company together with an experienced Talent Manager from Nova.
    • During this one hour session, we will guide you in how to plan and prepare for and the maximize value of your THS Armada participation and other career fairs.

  • CAREER SURVEY AND EVENT TOOL PRESENTATION – 1-2 H

    • Presentation of Universum career survey where you will get to know what previous year THS Armada students were looking for when choosing an employer.
    • We will go through different tips and event tools you can use to truly build your talent pool during the day.
